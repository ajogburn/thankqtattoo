MEET THE ARTIST

Qwami Tucker.
Owner of Thank Q Tattoos.

Resident at Bangarang Tattooing Company in Killeen, Texas.

Hey, I’m Qwami Tucker, owner of Thank Q Tattoos and a resident artist at Bangarang Tattooing Company in Killeen, Texas. Originally from San Diego, I bring a real passion and dedication to the craft.

I specialize in bold American Traditional work, but I’m just as comfortable with small precision pieces, custom illustrative designs, cover-ups, and pretty much any style you dream up. I love tattooing in all styles and forms. Every session is a chance to create something meaningful for you.

Whether it’s your first tattoo or the next piece in a full collection, you can expect honest communication, real professionalism, and ink that will stand the test of time.
