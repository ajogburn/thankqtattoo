I love tattooing in all styles and forms. My core is rooted in American Traditional — strong lines, rich color, and imagery that carries weight — but I approach every piece with curiosity and respect for the client’s vision.

Whether it’s a small meaningful symbol, a full custom backpiece, or breathing new life into an old tattoo through a cover-up, I believe the best work happens when artist and client collaborate with honesty and clear communication.

I want you to leave not only with incredible ink, but with an experience that feels personal and intentional.
