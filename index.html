WHAT I DO

Specialties & Approach

I love tattooing in all styles and forms. My foundation is American Traditional — bold lines, saturated color, and timeless imagery — but I bring the same care and intensity to small work, custom pieces, and challenging cover-ups.

EXPLORE SERVICES & BOOK
American Traditional

Classic bold linework, vibrant palettes, and iconic imagery: roses, daggers, eagles, panthers, anchors, and more. Built to age beautifully.

Small Pieces & Jammers

Compact, high-impact tattoos. Perfect for first pieces, meaningful symbols, or adding to existing collections without huge commitment.

Custom & Illustrative

One-of-a-kind designs created with you. Flash and predrawn options available, or fully custom work from your idea or reference.

Cover-ups & Transformations

Thoughtful cover-ups and rework. I’ll help you turn old ink you no longer connect with into something powerful and intentional.
