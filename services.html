Aftercare (Summary)
  • Leave the initial bandage on for up to 4-6 hours (or until bedtime).
  • Wash gently with antibacterial soap and warm water using only your hands. Pat dry with clean paper towel.
  • For the first ~24 hours: Re-apply a pea-sized amount of Armor Gel (if advised), then wrap with sterile saran wrap every 4-6 hours. Do not leave wrapped longer than 8 hours without cleaning.
  • After the first day/night: Keep clean and dry. No more wrapping.
  • Expect peeling/flaking and itching around days 3–7. This is normal healing — do not pick.
  • Use only a very small amount of unscented lotion (Eucerin or Aquaphor) if needed for dryness/itch.
  • Keep out of direct sun and no swimming/submersion for at least 2–3 weeks.
  • After 2 weeks, use zinc-based sunblock daily to protect the ink.
