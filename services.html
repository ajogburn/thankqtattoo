THE PROCESS

Services & Booking

Consultations are required for all new projects. I work by appointment only and take the time to understand exactly what you want before we begin.

CORE SPECIALTY
American Traditional
  • • Bold linework & saturated color
  • • Classic motifs (rose, dagger, eagle, panther, anchor, skull, etc.)
  • • Flash & custom adaptations
  • • Work designed to age gracefully
Small Pieces & Jammers

Compact, powerful tattoos ideal for wrists, fingers, ribs, behind the ear, or anywhere you want something meaningful without a large commitment. Great first tattoos.

Custom & Illustrative

Bring your references or ideas. I design from scratch or adapt your vision into something that will translate beautifully as permanent ink. Predrawn flash available too.

Cover-ups & Reworks

Thoughtful transformations of existing tattoos. Not every old tattoo can be completely erased, but almost everything can be dramatically improved. We’ll discuss realistic options.

Studio Policies
Consultations

Required for all new clients and larger projects. This can be done in person at the shop or via text/DM with good reference photos and a clear description.

Deposits

A non-refundable deposit ($80–150 depending on project size) is required to secure your appointment date. It is applied toward the final cost of the tattoo.

Cancellations & Reschedules

Please give at least 48 hours notice. Deposits for no-shows or late cancellations are forfeited. Multiple cancellations may affect future booking priority.

Pricing

Custom work is priced by the piece. Small pieces start at a minimum. You’ll receive a quote after we discuss size, placement, and complexity during consultation.

Aftercare

Detailed aftercare instructions are provided in person and via text. I’m available for follow-up questions as your tattoo heals.
